Movie Together

MovieTogether will recommend the most possibly interesting movies for the registered movie chasers. Meanwhile, our application will also push the nearby people who have the same interests on the same movie.

Feature One

(Movie recommendation): Based on the movies that you have watched, MovieTogether would recommend new movies for you. Think about the embarrassment when you finally have a hard-won Saturday night which you really want some real fun to spend the good time. It is great you do not to waste the time to find ideal movies. It is even greater that you do not need to bear the risk to waste your rare unoccupied Saturday night with some thing you do not really like. Just use MoviewTogether and enjoy! Let MovieTogether solve all these problems for you. MoviewTogether will use machine learning algorithm to analyze your history and predict the potential movies you will like and suggest them to you.
